If your certificate is from Fiji, Hong Kong or Israel

Fiji, Hong Kong and Israel send their police certificates direct to us. If your police certificate is from one of those territories, upload evidence you have applied for it, such as a receipt. Enter the date of the receipt in the issue date field.
